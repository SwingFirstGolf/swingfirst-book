How it works

Simple by design. Yours all season.

A Standing Tee Membership is a brand new concept — instead of booking a tee time each week and hoping for availability, you own a recurring slot that is yours every other week for the entire season. Same day, same time, same group. No app, no lottery, no last-minute scramble. You simply show up.

01
Choose your group
Select Group A (starts week of Nov 9) or Group B (starts week of Nov 16). Each group plays every other week for 12 rounds through April.
02
Pick your tee time
Choose the day and time that fits your life. Your exact 12 play dates are shown before you commit. Book as a twosome or foursome.
03
Pay once for the season
One payment locks in your tee time for all 12 rounds. No re-booking, no lottery, no uncertainty — just show up on your day and play.
04
Keep it for next season
Winter members get first access to summer. Summer members secure their winter slot for the following year — the loyalty loop that rewards staying in.

Membership policy

Tee times are non-transferable.

One player can't make it?
Any tee time owner may invite a guest to fill that spot at no charge. Guests play as the personal guest of the member.
No owners can make it?
The tee time may not be sold, transferred, or given to a non-member. It simply goes unplayed that week.
At least one owner must play
A named tee time owner must be present at every round. The membership cannot be used solely by guests.
